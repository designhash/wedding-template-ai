How It Happened

Our Love Story

2019 · Spring

The First Hello

A mutual friend's rooftop party. She was telling a story so vividly that everyone laughed. He couldn't take his eyes off her. They talked until the city lights faded.

2019 · Summer

The First Date

A tiny ramen shop, a two-hour wait, and the best conversation of both their lives. They missed the last train home and didn't mind one bit.

2020 · Autumn

Moving In Together

Amid a global pause, they built a cozy world of their own — mismatched furniture, shared playlists, and a window box of herbs neither of them could keep alive.

2022 · Winter

Kyoto in the Snow

Under a bamboo grove dusted in white, he got down on one knee. She said yes before he could finish the question. A passing monk bowed approvingly.

2026 · Spring

Forever Begins

And now, surrounded by everyone they love, they choose each other — again and always. The greatest adventure starts here.
